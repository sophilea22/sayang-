22 Things I Love About You

There are a million reasons I love you. But here are 22 that I can put into words.

01
I love the way you stay calm when I overthink everything.
02
I love how you listen — even to my longest, most random stories.
03
I love the way you care about the smallest things.
04
I love how you try to understand me instead of judging me.
05
I love your patience with my mood swings.
06
I love how safe I feel when I’m with you.
07
I love that you’re strong, but still soft with me.
08
I love your laugh — especially when it’s genuine and loud.
09
I love how you motivate me without pressuring me.
10
I love the way you remember little details about me.
11
I love how you protect what we have.
12
I love your ambition and how you work hard for your future.
13
I love how you make ordinary days feel special.
14
I love how you handle problems instead of running away.
15
I love the way you look at me when you think I don’t notice.
16
I love how you accept my flaws and stay anyway.
17
I love your random jokes, even the bad ones.
18
I love how you try to fix things when we argue.
19
I love that you choose me — not just once, but every day.
20
I love how you make me feel wanted and important.
21
I love the growth I see in you.
22
And most of all, I love who I am when I’m with you — because you make me feel safe enough to be my real self.

And if I had to choose again, in every lifetime, I would still choose you.

Forever, Yours.
